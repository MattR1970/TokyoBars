Guide to Shinjuku Ni-chome's Bars

Café Lavandería
Known for its relaxed, activist vibe, Café Lavandería is not just a bar but a cultural hub where discussions and music nights are common. Enjoy a drink and perhaps engage in conversations about global and local issues. RATING: Highly rated.
View Map
Campy! Bar
This bar is vibrant and colorful, known for its flamboyant decor and drag queen staff. Everyone tries their best to speak English, making it welcoming for tourists. RATING: Popular.
View Map
Eagle Tokyo
Styled with a Western aesthetic, this bar is popular among the bear community and is known for its friendly, multilingual staff. The atmosphere is reminiscent of a Brooklyn lounge. RATING: Favored.
View Map
New Sazae
A Tokyo nightlife institution, New Sazae offers a nostalgic disco experience from the 1970s. Known for its lively atmosphere, it's a fantastic place to dance the night away. RATING: Esteemed.
View Map
King Tokyo
Highly popular and inclusive, King Tokyo is celebrated for its vibrant community, great music, and curated cocktails. It's a place where you can easily make new friends and enjoy an energetic night out. RATING: Celebrated.
